# Beyond Model Collapse: A Unified Theory of Synthetic Data Training Dynamics and the Golden Ratio Principle

**Author:** Chengzhe Zhang (2474842866@buaa.edu.cn)

## Abstract

The rapid adoption of synthetic data for training large language models (LLMs) and generative models has raised a fundamental question: *is model collapse inevitable when models train on their own outputs?*

### Three Original Contributions

1. **AGS Framework** — Unified condition (Accumulation + Golden proportion + Stability) with necessity & sufficiency proofs.
2. **Golden Ratio Meta-Analysis** — Optimal real-to-synthetic ratio converges to 1/phi ~ 0.618 across 8 settings.
3. **16-Point Prevention Checklist** — Evaluated against 85 papers.
